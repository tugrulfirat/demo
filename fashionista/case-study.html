Fashionista — Conversion-Focused Luxury E‑commerce Website (UI/UX + Design System)

“Final homepage (grid-focused) — luxury aesthetic with conversion-first hierarchy.”

Overview

Designed a luxury fashion e‑commerce experience for a client under NDA, balancing premium brand feel with clear purchase paths. The goal was to increase conversion by improving first-impression clarity, product discovery, and checkout confidence.

“Above-the-fold: clear value proposition, primary CTA, and immediate product context.”
“Key conversion risks addressed: hierarchy, CTA clarity, and decision support.”

The Challenge

Luxury e‑commerce often looks premium but loses customers through:

  • unclear hierarchy and weak CTAs
  • slow product discovery (too much browsing friction)
  • checkout uncertainty (shipping/returns/payment clarity)

Outcome I targeted: reduce hesitation and increase “add to cart” and checkout completion through clarity and momentum.

Goals (Conversion-Driven)

  • Make the offer understandable within 5 seconds (what it is, who it’s for, why it’s worth it)
  • Improve product scanning and category entry
  • Increase PDP → Add to Cart intent through clearer product presentation
  • Reduce checkout drop-off with step clarity and reassurance

Conversion Metrics

Translated into concrete UX requirements.

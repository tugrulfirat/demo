THE NORDIC RETREAT

The Vision

RECLAIMING
SERENITY

The Nordic Retreat was born from a desire to create a sanctuary of absolute calm amidst the energetic pace of urban life. Our client requested a space that felt both expansive and intimate—a delicate balance achieved through honest materials and intentional light management.

Every element, from the hand-brushed oak flooring to the custom architectural lighting, was selected to provoke a sense of grounding and presence.

Location Stockholm, Sweden
Style Scandinavian Minimalism
Services Full Interior, Custom Furniture
Area 220 m²

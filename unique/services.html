Common Questions

FAQs

What is your typical project timeline? +
Timelines vary depending on the scope. A full residential design typically takes 4-9 months from discovery to reveal, while furnishing projects can range from 3-6 months.
Do you work on projects outside of the city? +
Yes, we take on select projects nationally and internationally. For distant projects, we utilize a hybrid model of site visits and virtual coordination.
How do you charge for your services? +
We typically work on a combination of a fixed project fee (based on scope) and a percentage-based procurement fee for furnishings and materials.
Can you work with my existing architect? +
Absolutely. We find that the best results come from early collaboration between architects and interior designers to ensure interior and exterior speak the same language.

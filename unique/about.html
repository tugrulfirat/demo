Our Story

THE ART OF
INTENTION

Founded by Eleanor Vance, UNIQUE began with a simple observation: the most beautiful spaces aren't just seen—they are felt. Our approach is rooted in the "Slow Design" movement, emphasizing quality over quantity and the beauty of natural imperfections.

Over the past seven years, we've had the privilege of transforming high-end residences and commercial spaces into timeless statements of identity. We don't follow trends; we create environments that tell your story.

ELEANOR VANCE, FOUNDER

Workflow

THE PROCESS

01

DISCOVERY & VISION

We begin by diving deep into your lifestyle, preferences, and aspirations. This phase is about understanding the "why" behind your project to establish a solid conceptual foundation.

02

DESIGN DEVELOPMENT

Here, we translate vision into reality through mood boards, floor plans, and material selections. We iterate until every detail feels aligned with the project's soul.

03

IMPLEMENTATION

Our studio handles the complex orchestration of procurement, coordination with contractors, and site supervision. We manage the overwhelm so you don't have to.

04

STYLING & REVEAL

The final layer—furniture, art, and accessories—is curated to bring the space to life. We deliver a turnkey environment ready for you to inhabit and enjoy.
